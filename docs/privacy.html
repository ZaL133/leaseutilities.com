Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains the data we collect, how it's used, and your data privacy rights.

Use of Local Storage

We use browser local storage to enhance your user experience. Local storage allows our website to remember information about your visit, so you don't have to re-enter it the next time you visit. This might include information like your lease start date.

Please note: This data is stored only on your device and is not collected or stored by our servers.

Google Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics, a service which transmits website traffic data to Google servers. Google Analytics does not identify individual users or associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. We use reports provided by Google Analytics to help us understand website traffic and webpage usage.
